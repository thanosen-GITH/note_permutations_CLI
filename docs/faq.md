# FAQ

## How many notes can I use?

Between 1 and 12.

## Why is this so slow for large numbers?

Permutation counts grow factorially. This is expected and unavoidable.

## Can I browse permutations interactively?

Not currently, but the algorithm is designed to support this in a future GUI.
